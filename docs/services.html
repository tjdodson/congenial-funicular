Our Services

We like to think of ourselves as a special ops team for our clientele. Studio 54x aims to accommodate the needs of all our clients. For special circumstances, availability, and/or additional information, please contact us for a consultation.

Event Sessions Include:

  • Weddings
  • Corporate Events
  • Galas

We've become quite renowned for our event photography, so much so that we needed to develop separate event and wedding packages. Session costs start at $400, depending on the number of shots, overall session duration, and the number of subjects.

Our most basic package begins at 50 edited proofs; speak with a consultant to see how much it would cost for your perfect session.

If the location is greater than 20 miles' distance from the studio, there is an additional cost for travel.
